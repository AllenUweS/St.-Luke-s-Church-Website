About Us

Established in 1998
St. Luke's Church, a cherished church within the Karnataka Northern Diocese of the Church of South India,
has been a beacon of faith, hope, and community since its establishment in the year 1998.
Situated in the heart of Karnataka, our church has played a pivotal role in nurturing the spiritual well-being of our congregation and serving the broader community.
